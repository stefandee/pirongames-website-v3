Play That Word Game (released on 24.11.2008)

 
 
 

Description

Where words and physics collide!

Help

Controls:

  • click a letter to add to the word;
  • click the last letter to deselect it;
  • double-click the last letter or press 'Enter' to submit the word;
  • press 'Backspace' to delete the last letter;
  • press 'Delete' to erase the entire word;
  • press 'P' to pause.

Game Modes:

  • 'Endless': survive as long as you can and try to make words with 6 or more letters;
  • '10 Minutes to Midnight': try to score as high as you can in 10 minutes;
  • 'Ladder': combine all letters into words as the difficulty increases;
  • 'Pit Cleaner': 65 letters dropped. Can you find all the words?
  • 'Alphabet Soup': compose the alphabet as fast as you can.
